## FAQ

### Is this a subscription?

No. The $19 price is a one-time payment for lifetime access to the current Party Quest Maker web app.

### Do I need an account?

No. The app works without an account. Purchase verification is handled through Stripe.

### Where are my saved quests stored?

Saved quests remain in the browser on the device where they were created. They are not automatically synced to another browser or device.

### Can I print the games?

Yes. Lifetime access includes printable quest materials designed for home or event use.

### Can I use the app for children?

The app includes child-friendly age settings, but an adult should review the generated game and supervise play. The host must confirm that clues and locations are safe and suitable.

### Can I resell generated packs?

No. The purchase is for the buyer's personal or internal event use. Access credentials, generated packs, and app output may not be resold, redistributed, or offered as a competing product.

### What happens if I change devices?

Browser-local saves do not automatically transfer. Keep printable copies of games you want to retain. Purchase recovery may require the original checkout details.

### Are refunds available?

Because access is delivered digitally, refund requests are reviewed according to the marketplace's rules and applicable consumer law. Contact support with the order email and a description of the issue.
